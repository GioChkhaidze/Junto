# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Hosts running live class or study discussions and participants answering a short, timed question set in a shared room.

## Product Purpose

Junto collects room-local answers, analyzes which ideas and approaches they contain, and forms discussion groups with the strongest feasible collective coverage.

## Brand Personality

Clear, academic, and composed. The interface should feel trustworthy in a bright seminar room and move people through a live activity without ceremony.

## Anti-references

No glossy AI dashboard language, chat-first interface, decorative data cards, gradients, glass effects, chips, or novelty controls. Do not imitate a social feed or a game-show scoreboard.

## Design Principles

- Keep the current task unmistakable.
- Show saved, timed, and submitted states plainly.
- Use familiar education-product structure without copying a specific product.
- Reserve green for direction, action, and success.
- Make host and participant views legible from across a classroom and on a laptop.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA, full keyboard operation, visible focus, non-color state cues, reduced-motion support, and responsive layouts down to narrow phone widths even though the primary product is a website.
