# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Room hosts and participants use Junto during classes, study sessions, workshops, and idea-matching activities. A host
creates one anonymous room; participants join by code, answer independently under a shared deadline, and receive their
discussion group without creating an account.

## Product Purpose

Junto forms discussion groups whose members collectively provide the strongest feasible coverage of the activity's
required ideas and productive perspectives. Success means the room can move from questions to defensible groups quickly,
while showing honestly what the semantic evaluator inferred and what the optimizer could or could not guarantee.

## Brand Personality

Calm, rigorous, and collegial. The interface should feel immediately familiar under classroom time pressure and keep
attention on writing, facilitation, and the resulting discussion agenda.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble an account-heavy institutional dashboard, a noisy quiz show, or an ornamental AI product. Avoid chips,
gradients, glass effects, celebratory clutter, leaderboards, fake progress, promotional AI language, and decorative card
grids. Canvas, Piazza, and SAIL are references for academic clarity and dependable task structure, not for visual
copying.

## Design Principles

1. Room before identity: access and identity are anonymous and scoped to one room.
2. Thinking before grouping: participants answer independently before the cohort is composed.
3. One decision at a time: sequential workflows beat dashboards and configuration walls.
4. Explain results honestly: distinguish model judgments, solver guarantees, feasibility, and uncertainty.
5. Let the interface disappear: familiar controls, restrained hierarchy, and precise copy serve the classroom task.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard operation, visible focus, 44-pixel touch targets, reduced motion, narrow-screen
reflow, semantic labels and headings, live announcements, and state indicators that do not depend on color alone.
